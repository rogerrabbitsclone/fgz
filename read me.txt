FGZ (Flash GameZ)
launches flash games (swf) locally. 

the best way to get flash games is to open up a game in your browser, view the source of that page, search for "swf" in the page to get a physical link to the swf. save the swf from that and drop in the "games folder". it will be apparent where that is once you run the program.
